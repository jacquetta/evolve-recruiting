About Evolve Recruiting

What is Evolve Recruiting?

I have been recruiting for over 6 years in the professional setting but have always been a coach to my friends and family in their job search and resume needs. What made me want to take on this journey and expand my services was my ability to want to see others succeed. I have always been the person that was a motivator and coach to anyone that needed a helping hand. Evolve recruiting was always a dream, something that I thought I couldn't do but wanted to. How can someone that motivates others not motivate themself? So I prayed and God answered my prayers, he told me my path and where I should go next and that is to build Evolve Recruiting and Coaching Firm into an organization that thrives on seeing other people succeed. As a black owned small business I am dedicating my time and energy into building up other small businesses that are looking to expand and grow their staff. My company is building people up, providing services to the community with workshops and also holding events for employers to come and connect. Evolve Recruiting and Coaching is taking the job search and job placement process to the next level.

Who is Liddy Pearson?

My name is Arlidian Pearson, I prefer Liddy. Mom of 1 daughter, Amarei and a dog names Dexter. Based in Tampa, FL but born in Greensboro,NC Backstory: Growing up I was the motivator and positive person amongst friends and I even was voted the most likely to be a motivational speaker in 10th grade.
