Evolve Recruiting Mission, Values, & Objectives

To connect the unemployed to open positions and allowing for employers to have a direct connection to quality candidates.

  • Honesty, Transparency, & Professionalism
  • Community, Small Business, & Transition
  • Job Growth, Success, Opportunity
  • Coaching, Motivating, & Encouraging Job Seekers
  • To always achieve the expectations of the client
  • To engage the community and advocate for the job seekers
  • To search for new ways to connect employers and candidates
  • To be a valued employer and job seeker resource
